Overview

NASequencer is a text-based MIDI composition App. It focuses on quick composing and easy management. There have been several apps with musical notation for generating music sequence. Generally, plain-text is easy to edit and manage, however it can often be unreadable in music composition. NASequencer assists you to read structure of music composition by real-time previewing with piano roll and event list. Additionally, it assists you to check sound of ensemble by SoundFont synthesizer.
